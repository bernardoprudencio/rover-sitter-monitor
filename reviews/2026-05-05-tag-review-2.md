## Headline finding: the `cat` cascade

The new `Safety and aggression incidents` and `Lost or injured pet` problems work — but their keyword lists are dog-centric. Cat-incident posts (cat bite, cat fight, cat died, cat escaped) are firing **only** on the `cat` keyword and end up in `Cats and kittens` (a rate-preference category) when they should be in `Clients` incident categories.

Posts found:
- "Cat died in my care" → tagged `Cats and kittens`, should be `Lost or injured pet`
- "Cat bite from boarding" → tagged `Cats and kittens`, should be `Safety and aggression incidents`
- "Cat fight advice" → same
- "UPDATE: Extremely Aggressive Cat" → same
- "One of my cat clients died" → same

**Single fix unlocks all of these:** generalize the new problems' keywords beyond dogs (see sidecar).

## Missing-tag opportunities (keyword gaps)

### Add cat/general-pet variants to `Safety and aggression incidents` and `Lost or injured pet`

**Safety and aggression incidents** — add: `cat bite`, `cat fight`, `aggressive cat`, `pet attack`, `animal bite`, `trust and safety`

**Lost or injured pet** — add: `lost cat`, `lost dog`, `cat escaped`, `darted out`, `cat died`, `dog died`, `passed away`, `put to sleep`, `client died`, `stopped eating`, `neglect`

### Broaden phrasings on the new problems

**Rover Support quality** — current keywords missed "lack of response", "live chat" with rover, "rover trust [and safety]". Add: `lack of response`, `live chat`, `trust and safety`, `rover trust`, `no answer from rover`, `rover never responded`.

**Account suspension / deactivation** — missed "blocked by Rover for 7 days". Add: `blocked by rover`, `blocked from rover`, `rover blocked me`, `account blocked`.

**Difficult clients** — missed posts where owners *accused* sitters of abuse / asked sitters to *pay for damage*. Add: `client accused me`, `owner accused me`, `owner thinks i`, `pay for damage`, `blamed me`.

1. **Remove `cash` from `Going off app`** — biggest single false-positive remaining.
2. **Narrow `zoom` / `bird` / `24/7` / `stars`** — small phrase swaps.

5. **Run `--retag` again** and spot-check the previously-flagged posts to confirm they now have the right tags.

After this round, an honest representative-sample audit (not biased toward false positives) would give a baseline accuracy number for the dashboard.
